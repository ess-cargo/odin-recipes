Air Fryer Chicken

Description

This recipe is adapted from allrecipes.com: Air Fryer Blackened Chicken Breasts

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Serving size: 1 chicken breast; recipe makes 2
  • Macros per serving: 245 cal, 7g fat, 3g carbs, 40g protein

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. paprika
  • 1 tsp. ground thyme
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • .5 tsp onion powder
  • .5 tsp black pepper
  • .25 tsp salt
  • 2. tsp. vegetable oil
  • 2 6-oz. skinless boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a gallon Ziploc bag.
  2. Coat the chicken breasts in the marinade.
  3. Let the chicken sit and preheat the air fryer to 360 degrees F.
  4. Set the chicken in the air fryer and cook for about 20 minutes. Flip halfway through.
  5. Chicken must be no longer pink and juices must run clear. An instant-read thermometer should read at least 165 degrees F.
  6. Transfer chicken to a plate and let rest 5 minutes before serving.
