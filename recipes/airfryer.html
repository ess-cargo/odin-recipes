Air Fryer Baked Potato

Description

This recipe is adapted from allrecipes.com: Easy Air Fryer Baked Potatoes

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 40 minutes
  • Serving size: 1 potato; recipe makes 4
  • Macros per serving: 448 cal, 19g fat, 65g carbs, 8g protein

Ingredients

  • 4 large baking potatoes, scrubbed
  • 2 TB olive oil
  • kosher salt and black pepper to taste
  • .5 tsp. garlic powder, or to taste
  • .5 tsp. dried parsley, or to taste
  • 4 TB butter

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F.
  2. Combine ingredients in a gallon Ziploc bag.
  3. Coat potatoes with olive oil and seasonings.
  4. Place potatoes in the air fryer basket and cook until potatoes are soft, 40-50 minutes.
  5. Slice potatoes lengthwise and fill each with 1 TB butter.
  6. Add additional toppings as desired and serve.
