Cucumber Salad

Description

This recipe is adapted from allrecipes.com: Cucumber Salad

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Marinate time: 1 hour (Salad can be marinated overnight)
  • Recipe makes 8 servings
  • Macros per serving: 99 cal, 0g fat, 25g carbs, 1g protein

Ingredients

  • 4 thinly-sliced cucumbers with half-peeled skins
  • thinly sliced white onion or shallot
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • .75 cup white sugar
  • .5 cup water
  • Dried dill to taste

Steps

  1. Toss sliced cucumbers and onion together in large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Combine vinegar, sugar, and water in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil.
  3. Pour mixture over cucumbers and onions in the bowl. Stirl in dill.
  4. Cover salad and let marinate in the refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving.
